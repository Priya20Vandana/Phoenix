WHY FOLLOW DIFFERENT COLOR PALETTES?

When creating a capsule wardrobe picking out a cohesive color palette is one of the most helpful things you can do to ensure that your capsule experience will go smoothly.

Not only do you want to pick colors that go together, but you also want to pick colors that complement the color of your hair, eyes and skin tone. With that in mind, we've created 12 different color palettes based on that criteria so that your capsule plays up what makes you, YOU.

Not sure where to start?

we're here to help!

BUBBLY BLUES

We Can’t Get Enough of This Color, and Neither Can Designers. The vibrant hue packs a bit more punch than, say, a traditional navy shade, yet it still has the ability to seamlessly mix and match with other items in your closet.

I PURPLE YOU

Since purple is extremely versatile, it's essentially the perfect color for transitional weather. There's nothing stopping you from layering a cardigan over your bright purple summer dress, or rocking purple pants as a way to spice up your 50-degree outfits.

PASTEL PINKS

Love it or loathe it, hot pink is a color trend with legs. From fluorescent pink to fuchsia, this bold, aggressive color has become deeply embedded in the American psyche as a shorthand for all things both feminine and femme.

GO WILD WITH GREEN

From olive to mint, emerald to Kermit, you need some green in your wardrobe. Green made fashion headlines last year when Bottega green was pronounced the new black.

BELLE IN BLUE

This bright blue hue has been trending on the runway for some time now and has finally made its way to the streets. Tackle two trends in one with Everlane’s tie-dyed tank top.

LAVENDER WITH LOVE

This is soon going to be a summer wardrobe essential outfit that would be loved by one and all. This dress is so easy-breezy and super attractive that you might not even have to make efforts to style it.

BOLD IN BROWN

Brown is having a renaissance. From deep chocolate to buttery toffee, brunette hues have returned from fashion exile to become one of the most sought-after shades of the year.

BABYPINK BLUSH

For a doll-like look, baby pink takes on baby doll dress with balloon sleeves, the ultimate trend of the season, much like the one worn by Emili Sindlev .

RAVISHING RED

Of all the colors, red is likely the most prominent. A dominant color in most religious ceremonies, red has an array of positive connotations in Indian culture.

THE SCARLET WEDDING

Nothing can beat the charm of a beautiful scarlet lehenga. No matter how many trends we see in pastels and nude shades, a bride in scarlet looks exceptionally gorgeous!

PHENOMENAL IN PINK

Known as the sweetest color in the color palette, "Pink" is one such hue that pleases our eyes and ensnares our senses too deep. Such is the unmissable charm of this happy color.

SHADES OF WHITE

For the purity and subtleness that it adds, the pristine color white (ivory) is an immensely incorporated hue in modern Indian weddings. A predominantly seen color in western weddings, the color white lends a graceful charm, unlike any other color which makes it a top favored color for Hindu weddings as well.
