"Fashion should be a form of escapism, and not a form of imprisonment."

India is a very big country which a wide variety of culture and customs present. This country has Himalaya Mountains to its north to Arabian Sea to its south. So to suit such varying climatic conditions we could found different types of traditional clothing worn by people of India. Indian clothing has found its place even in international ramps.Indian wardrobes also vary from one state to another, from one region to another. Every place has its unique clothes and style.

Let the Indian-ness show!
(Through these ethnic collections)

Channel Regal Vibes In Traditional Velvet Wear


Alia Bhatt’s Indian wear wardrobe is full of standout ethnic looks that are perfect for a wedding-hopping millennial. Her recent jaw dropping outfit by Sabyasachi comprised of a deep red silk kurta paired with a regal velvet ghagra that added an old school touch to her overall sultry ensemble.

Style Tip: Opt for a deep red velvety colour like the actors to add a royal charm to your overall ensemble.

Girls, Go Shine In Sweeping Sequined Traditional Saris


Janhvi Kapoor gives a party-ready update to the traditional six yards of elegance silhouette by donning a stunning lilac hued sequin sari by Manish Malhotra that definitely goes for a ‘Who’s that girl?’ vibe. Having a friend's cocktail party and want to steal the spotlight? This ensemble is definitely your when in doubt go-to.

Style Tip: Drape your sari in a low waist look like the actor, to look extra sexy.

Flowy Floral Traditional Numbers Are Perfect For Summer Wedding Festivities


Once again covered in pretty pink blooms, Katrina Kaif's breezy floral lehenga by Sabyasachi defines the less-is-more aesthetic in the most feminine way possible, making it a perfect fit for the sultry season.

Style Tip: Opt for prints with fun and vibrant blooms like the actors to make a sultry fashion statement in style.

Traditional Lehenga Co-ords Sets Are A Winner


When it comes to blending stylish Indian wear with comfort, Aditi Rao Hydari knows how to put her best foot forward. This time around, she opted for a tropical, leaf printed coordinate lehenga set which comes with a cape to add extra oomph to her overall ensemble.

Style Tip: Throw on a lightweight contrasting cape upon your coordinate set like the actors to look extra chic this summer season.

Go For The Traditional Pantone Path All The Way


There’s nothing like a bright and happy hue like yellow to elevate your ethnic look for the summer. Alia Bhatt borrowed the pantone colour of the year and made a ravishing style statement all the way in a stunning A-line kurta set, and honestly, we’re definitely loving every bit of it.

Style Tip: An edgy A-line number like the actors will go a long way along with complimenting your body type..

Dramatic Ruffled Fit Traditional Dresses Have Our Hearts


Sonakshi Sinha surely painted the town a romantic red in her jaw dropping ruffled sari by Arpita Mehta, that is to die for. The actors' flowy number was enough to add the extra factor to the overall dramatic look.

Style Tip: Add easy-breezy ruffles like the actors to your staple traditional silhouettes to stand out in the crowd, this wedding season.

Make Statement In Traditional Style With Layering


Sonam Kapoor looks drop dead gorgeous in an edgy silk brocade Kurti in a sunny yellow colour, smartly layered up with a bold green kimono style shrug that features intricate prints all over. Embracing Indian wear like no one else the actor adds a unique twist to her staple kurti set making for a killer fashion statement.

Style Tip: Layer up your staple kurta sets with statement jackets to obtain an unconventional traditional look.
